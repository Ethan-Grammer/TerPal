BCHM461
Biochemistry I
Syllabus Repository (2)
Credits: 3
Grad Meth : Reg, P-F, Aud
Prerequisite: Minimum grade of C- in CHEM271 and CHEM272; or minimum grade of C- in CHEM276 and CHEM277.

BCHM464
Biochemistry Laboratory
Syllabus Repository (0)
Credits: 3
Grad Meth : Reg, P-F, Aud

Restriction: BCHM, CHEM, and Nutritional Sciences majors have first priority, followed by other life science majors.

BCHM661
Nucleic Acids I
Syllabus Repository (1)
Credits: 2
Grad Meth : Reg, Aud
Prerequisite: BSCI410 and BCHM465.
Topics covered: Chemistry and structure of DNA and RNA, from nucleotides to chromatin, chromosomes, and genomes, and some methods for studying, synthesizing, sequencing and manipulating nucleic acids. Rudimentary genomics and bioinformatics. DNA Biology: selected aspects of the biochemistry and regulation of DNA replication, repair, and recombination, and how these processes interact with each other.

BCHM669A
Special Topics in Biochemistry; Methods for Biomolecular Structure Determination in Solution
Syllabus Repository (0)
Credits: 3
Grad Meth : Reg, Aud
Introduction to modern methods for determining structures of biological macromolecules in solution, ranging from low-resolution methods, such as small-angle X-ray and neutron scattering (SAXS/SANS), to high-resolution nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR). The course covers detailed theoretical background and applications of these methods to study biomolecular structure and dynamics and macromolecular interactions.
